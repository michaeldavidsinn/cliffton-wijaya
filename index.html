About Me

Hi, I'm Cliffton Wijaya.

a student-athlete at National Tsing Hua University, Taiwan who splits his time between hitting shots on the court and capturing moments behind the camera. I make content about vlogs, uni life, and whatever random things I stumble into along the way.

I like keeping things real. No heavy filters, no forced vibes, just life, the way it happens. One day I’m grinding at practice, the next day I’m just with friends making funny contents, and somehow it all turns into a story worth sharing. Basketball keeps me sharp, and creating keeps me inspired. I’m not trying to be perfect. Just consistent, authentic, and always improving. So whether it’s on the court, in class, or behind the camera, I’m focused on leveling up and enjoying the ride.
